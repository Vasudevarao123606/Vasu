## 💡 About the Project

**RecruitX** is a student-focused job information platform that brings together job opportunities from top companies into one clean, easy-to-use interface. Instead of hunting across multiple websites, students can visit RecruitX to find, search, and explore job listings — all in one place.

Every listing includes everything a student needs before applying — the role, company, location, salary range, eligibility criteria, a brief description, and the last date to apply. When ready, a single click takes them directly to the company's official career page.

---

## ❗ The Problem We're Solving

Students today struggle to keep track of job opportunities:

- 😓 Job postings are **scattered across** company websites, job boards, and social media
- 📋 Important details like **eligibility and deadlines** are often buried or missing
- ⏰ Students **miss application deadlines** due to lack of a centralized tracker
- 🔍 **No single platform** caters specifically to student-level opportunities
- 😵 Complex navigation makes the **job search process overwhelming**

RecruitX addresses all of this by centralizing job information in a format built for students — not recruiters.

## 🖥️ Pages

### 🏠 Home Page
The landing page of RecruitX. Features a prominent search bar, live stats showing total jobs and companies available, a scrolling companies section, and an About RecruitX section. Clean navigation bar across the top.

### 🔐 Login Page
A simple, student-friendly login screen. Enter any credentials to access the jobs section — no complex sign-up required.

### 💼 Jobs Page
The heart of the platform. All job listings are displayed as individual cards, each packed with full details and a direct **Apply** button that opens the company's official website.

## ⚙️ How It Works

Jobs are stored and generated within the application itself. When the jobs page loads, listings are dynamically rendered from a structured data set. The search bar and filters update results in **real time** as the student types or selects options. Login state is managed using browser storage so the session persists across page reloads.

## 🚫 Project Scope

This is a **frontend project** — it does not connect to a live database or backend server. All job data is self-contained within the application. The Apply button redirects students to official company websites for the actual application process.
